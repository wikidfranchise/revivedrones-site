WHEN PEOPLE NEED DRONES MOST

5G fails first.

Every scenario where autonomous drones save the most lives is a scenario where terrestrial networks collapse.

🔥

Wildfire

Cell towers burn. Power lines melt. 5G goes dark. But drones need to fly fire perimeters, find survivors, drop supplies. Satellite keeps flying.

🌊

Hurricane / Flood

Towers flood. Fiber cuts. Entire metro areas go offline for days. Maui 2023: cell service collapsed within hours. Drones on satellite flew.

⛰️

Tribal / Rural

No towers to begin with. The Navajo Nation has 27,000 sq mi and minimal cell coverage. These communities have the highest overdose death rates and the longest EMS response times. 5G will never reach them. Satellite already does.

🔌

Grid Failure

Texas 2021: the grid collapsed. Towers went dark in hours. Pipeline inspections, search and rescue, medical delivery — all impossible on cellular. Satellite runs on orbital solar.

🏔️

Infrastructure Corridors

Pipelines, power lines, rail — the highest-value BVLOS use cases run through terrain with zero cell coverage. You can't inspect what you can't connect to.

🚨

Mass Casualty Events

Cell networks overload in minutes during emergencies. Everyone calls 911 at once. Bandwidth collapses. Dedicated satellite capacity doesn't share spectrum with consumer phones.
