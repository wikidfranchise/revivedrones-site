About the Program

Revive Drones is a life-saving emergency drone delivery system designed to respond to opioid overdoses with Narcan deployment in real time. By texting a simple shortcode, witnesses or first responders can summon a drone to deliver the antidote within minutes. Drones can be autonomous and/or manned, depending on event.

Shortcode Activation

Users text 63911 "OD911" — triggering immediate dispatch of a Narcan-equipped drone to the location. Drones can hover to assist as needed.

Future payloads will include EpiPens, insulin, and AEDs for rapid crisis response.
