About the Program

Revive Drones™ is a life-saving emergency drone delivery system designed to respond to opioid overdoses with nasal spray deployment in real time. By texting a simple shortcode, witnesses or first responders can summon a drone to deliver the aid within minutes. Drones can be autonomous and/or manned, depending on the event.

Shortcode Activation

Users text 63911 with *OD911* — triggering immediate dispatch of a drone to the location. Drones can hover to assist as needed.

Future payloads will include EpiPens, insulin, and AEDs for rapid crisis response.
