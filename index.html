Revive Drones

Saving Lives from Above

Revive Drones deploys autonomous and semi-autonomous drones equipped with Narcan (naloxone) to opioid overdose events — in real time. Our system bridges the critical time gap between 911 calls and first responder arrivals.

Every second counts — our drones can reach scenes in under 3 minutes.

How It Works

  • Overdose reported by witness or EMS alert system
  • Drone launched with payload of Narcan (4mg spray)
  • Drone hovers above GPS-confirmed target and drops aid
  • Optional voice assist and video verification via onboard AI

Our Mission

With overdoses rising in urban, rural, and underserved areas, Revive Drones is committed to delivering life-saving treatments where traditional emergency response may fall short.
